HEBREW FONT PREVIEW - QUICK START GUIDE
========================================

You now have six static HTML files to preview different Hebrew fonts for your homepage.

STEP 1: VIEW THE PREVIEWS
--------------------------
Open each file in your web browser:
1. Double-click homepage_font1.html (Rubik - Modern & Clean)
2. Double-click homepage_font2.html (Heebo - Professional)
3. Double-click homepage_font3.html (Alef - Classic & Elegant)
4. Double-click homepage_font4.html (Assistant - Clean & Modern)
5. Double-click homepage_font5.html (Karantina - Bold & Distinctive)
6. Double-click homepage_font6.html (Frank Ruhl Libre - Traditional Serif)

Each page shows the same content with a different font applied to all headers and titles.

STEP 2: COMPARE THE FONTS
--------------------------
Look at these key areas on each page:
- Main hero title at the top
- Section headings ("איך זה עובד", "למה לבחור בנו")
- Card titles and descriptions
- Button text

Notice:
- Readability: Which is easiest to read?
- Brand fit: Which matches your brand personality?
- Professional look: Which looks most trustworthy?
- Visual hierarchy: Which creates the best distinction between elements?

STEP 3: MAKE YOUR CHOICE
-------------------------
Once you've decided which font works best for Printeam:
1. Note the font name (Rubik, Heebo, Alef, Assistant, Karantina, or Frank Ruhl Libre)
2. Share your selection with the development team
3. The chosen font will be integrated into the actual website

FONT CHARACTERISTICS:
---------------------
Rubik: Modern, rounded, friendly, contemporary
Heebo: Balanced, versatile, professional, neutral
Alef: Traditional, elegant, classic, timeless
Assistant: Clean, modern, readable, neutral
Karantina: Bold, distinctive, unique, eye-catching
Frank Ruhl Libre: Traditional, serif, sophisticated, elegant

All six fonts:
✓ Are designed specifically for Hebrew
✓ Are highly legible on all devices
✓ Load quickly from local files
✓ Are free for commercial use

Need help deciding? Consider:
- Your target audience (young/professional/traditional)
- Your brand values (modern/trustworthy/classic/fun)
- Your competitors' typography choices
- Overall website aesthetic
